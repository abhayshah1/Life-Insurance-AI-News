Colorado AI law keeps insurance decisioning controls in scope

Organization: Colorado General Assembly Date: compliance obligations active in 2026; source page for enacted SB24-205 High: primary regulatory source

Sourced facts: The Colorado bill summary states that developers and deployers of high-risk AI systems must use reasonable care to protect consumers from algorithmic discrimination, complete impact assessments, maintain risk management programs, provide notice when AI is a substantial factor in consequential decisions, and support appeal with human review where technically feasible.
